## Module 1: From Problem to Approach and From Requirements to Collection

### Course Introduction
- Describing how data scientists apply structured approach to solve business problems.
- Start creating own data science methodology stories

### Data Science Methodology Overview
**Consists of 10 Stages:**
- Business understanding
- Analytical approach
- Data requirements
- Data collection
- Data understanding
- Data preparation
- Modeling
- Evaluation
- Deployment
- Feedback

**Standard Data Science Methodology Questions**
- What is the problem you're trying to solve?
- How can you use data to answer the question?
- What data do you need to answer the question?
- Where's the data source from?
- How will you receive the data?
- Does the data you collect represent the problemt to be solved?
- What additional work is required to manipulate and work with the data?
- When you apply data visualisation, do you see the answer that address the business problem?
- Does the data model answer the initial business question, or must you adjust the data?
- Can you put the model into practice?
- Can you get constructive feedback from the data and the stakeholder to answer the business question?
